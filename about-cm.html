Our Story

In late 2023, driven by a shared passion for design and technology, Brian and Ian decided to take the leap and turn their vision into reality. Casper Maxus was born out of a desire to provide businesses with exceptional digital solutions that not only looked good but also delivered results. From humble beginnings in a small office in Nairobi, Casper Maxus quickly gained a reputation for its innovative approach to design and development. The agency's founders were committed to creating a culture that valued creativity, collaboration, and a relentless pursuit of excellence
